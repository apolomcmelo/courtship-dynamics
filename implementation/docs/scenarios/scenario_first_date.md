## SCENARIO 4 — FIRST DATE EXPERIENCE

### Variable Focus
Primary: Interaction quality during in-person meeting  
Secondary: Emotional dynamics  
Controlled: Appearance (varied but balanced)

### Context
You go on separate first dates with different people you have been interacting with.

### Situation
Each man represents a different type of first date experience.

### Character A
- Appearance: Attractive, stylish, confident presence
- Behavior: Builds strong emotional connection quickly, focuses heavily on you
- Dynamic: Intense beginning, gradually shifts toward self-focus
- Signal: Fast emotional bonding with instability risk

### Character B
- Appearance: Neat, approachable, calm and pleasant presence
- Behavior: Engaging, attentive, naturally interesting, balances humor and depth
- Dynamic: Conversation flows with ease and mutual engagement
- Signal: Stable and stimulating interaction

### Character C
- Appearance: Expressive, relaxed, slightly unconventional
- Behavior: Playful and entertaining, keeps things light
- Dynamic: Avoids deeper or serious topics
- Signal: Fun without depth

### Character D
- Appearance: Elegant but reserved style
- Behavior: Engages well initially but becomes distant when interaction deepens
- Dynamic: Subtle withdrawal from emotional closeness
- Signal: Difficulty sustaining intimacy

### Internal Archetypes
A: Narcissistic Charming  
B: Secure Balanced  
C: Adventurous Charismatic  
D: Avoidant Independent  

### Expected Interaction
- Rank:
  1. Best overall experience
  2. Who you would like to see again
  3. Who you would not meet again
