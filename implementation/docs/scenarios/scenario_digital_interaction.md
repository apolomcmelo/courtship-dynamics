## SCENARIO 2 — INSTAGRAM / DIGITAL INTERACTION

### Variable Focus
Primary: Communication style and engagement pattern  
Secondary: Emotional tone  
Controlled: Appearance (distributed attractiveness)

### Context
You follow some people on Instagram after seeing shared interests. Some begin interacting with your content.

### Situation
Four men initiate contact in different ways.

### Character A
- Appearance: Attractive, active lifestyle, visually appealing profile
- Behavior: Engages with energy, introduces topics, but disappears occasionally
- Response pattern: Intense → inconsistent
- Signal: High stimulation, low predictability

### Character B
- Appearance: Natural, well-kept photos, approachable and trustworthy aesthetic
- Behavior: Engages consistently, asks interesting questions, builds natural flow
- Response pattern: Stable and engaging
- Signal: Emotional stability with social intelligence

### Character C
- Appearance: Model-like aesthetics, very polished and curated images
- Behavior: Charismatic and flattering early on, mirrors your interests quickly
- Response pattern: Strong start → gradual self-focus
- Signal: Fast bonding with underlying self-orientation

### Character D
- Appearance: Attractive, casual, low-profile
- Behavior: Engages well initially, but avoids deeper topics or emotional direction
- Response pattern: Responsive but evasive in depth
- Signal: Emotional distance emerging over time

### Internal Archetypes
A: Adventurous Charismatic  
B: Secure Balanced  
C: Narcissistic Charming  
D: Avoidant Independent

- Rank:
  1. Who you would reply to first
  2. Who you would keep talking to
  3. Who you would lose interest in fastest
