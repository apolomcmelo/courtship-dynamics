## SCENARIO 3 — DATING APP (TINDER-LIKE)

### Variable Focus
Primary: Initial selection (profile + early behavior)  
Secondary: Intent signaling  
Controlled: Engagement level (all show interest)

### Context
You are using a dating app. You matched with several people and now you are deciding who to engage with.

### Situation
Four matches appear at the same time.

### Character A
- Appearance:  Decisive and sharp look, confident, well-composed photos
- Bio: Short, suggests social lifestyle and influence
- Behavior: Suggests meeting after brief interaction
- Signal: Status and decisiveness

### Character B
- Appearance: Warm, clear photos, friendly and neat presentation
- Bio: Thoughtful, shows personality and interests
- Behavior: Engages in meaningful conversation, builds connection gradually
- Signal: Stability and depth

### Character C
- Appearance: Creative, expressive, unconventional style, but still attractive
- Bio: Playful and unusual
- Behavior: Engages unpredictably, fluctuates in tone and focus
- Signal: Emotional variability

### Character D
- Appearance: Attractive, understated, natural presentation, minimal effort in a "cool" way
- Bio: Interesting but slightly vague
- Behavior: Engages well, but deflects when conversation becomes more personal or future-oriented
- Signal: Engagement with avoidance of depth

### Internal Archetypes
A: Social Dominant  
B: Secure Balanced  
C: Impulsive Disorganized  
D: Avoidant Independent  

### Expected Interaction
- Choose:
  1. Who you would message first
  2. Who you would accept a date from fastest
  3. Who you would deprioritize
