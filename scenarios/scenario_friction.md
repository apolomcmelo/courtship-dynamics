## SCENARIO 6 — LIGHT CONFLICT / FRICTION

### Variable Focus
Primary: Conflict response style  
Secondary: Emotional regulation  
Controlled: Appearance (balanced across all)

### Context
A small misunderstanding happens (e.g., delayed reply, change of plans).

### Situation
Each person reacts differently.

### Character A
- Appearance: Very attractive, well-dressed, confident posture
- Behavior: Becomes defensive and subtly shifts blame
- Dynamic: Protects ego instead of resolving the issue
- Signal: Low accountability

### Character B
- Appearance: Calm, approachable presence, simple but neat style, attractive
- Behavior: Addresses the issue directly, with clarity and calm tone
- Dynamic: Focuses on resolution and mutual understanding
- Signal: Emotional regulation and security

### Character C
- Appearance: Expressive, emotionally vivid presence
- Behavior: Reacts strongly, seeks reassurance
- Dynamic: Escalates emotional tone quickly
- Signal: High sensitivity

### Character D
- Appearance: Attractive, understated, neutral style
- Behavior: Engages initially but withdraws when tension increases
- Dynamic: Avoids sustained confrontation
- Signal: Conflict avoidance

### Internal Archetypes
A: Narcissistic Charming  
B: Secure Balanced  
C: Anxious Intense  
D: Avoidant Independent  

### Expected Interaction
- Rank:
  1. Who handles the situation best
  2. Who you would feel safest with
  3. Who you would avoid
