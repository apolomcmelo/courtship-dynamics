## SCENARIO 1 — BAR / SOCIAL ENVIRONMENT

### Variable Focus
Primary: Style of approach (social behavior)  
Secondary: Body language  
Controlled: Appearance (balanced attractiveness across all)

### Context
You are at a bar with live music. The atmosphere is relaxed, people are talking, laughing, and enjoying the night. You’re standing with a drink, observing the environment.

### Situation
Four men show clear interest in interacting with you. All are available, single, and open to conversation.

### Character A
- Appearance: Well-groomed, modern style, attractive but not exaggerated
- Behavior: Engages naturally with the environment, exchanges jokes with others before approaching you
- Body language: Relaxed, socially fluid, comfortable in the group
- Signal: Social status through environment validation

### Character B
- Appearance: Clean, well-groomed, pleasant and classic style
- Behavior: Approaches calmly and directly, asks thoughtful questions and listens attentively
- Body language: Grounded, present, respectful proximity
- Signal: Emotional presence and social intelligence

### Character C
- Appearance: Casual, expressive, slightly unconventional but attractive
- Behavior: Opens with humor and playful teasing, tries to create a fun interaction quickly
- Body language: Animated, energetic
- Signal: Spontaneity and emotional stimulation

### Character D
- Appearance: Highly attractive, polished and striking
- Behavior: Focuses intensely on you from the start, gives strong compliments and creates quick intimacy
- Body language: Direct, slightly intrusive proximity
- Signal: Fast emotional escalation

### Internal Archetypes
A: Social Dominant  
B: Secure Balanced  
C: Adventurous Charismatic  
D: Narcissistic Charming
